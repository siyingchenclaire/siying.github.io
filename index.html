About Me

I'm a Master's student in Speech and Language Processing at the University of Edinburgh, with a strong foundation in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing, and Speech Processing.

Currently collaborating with Dialpad on my dissertation project, where I leverage large datasets to improve automatic speech recognition (ASR) accuracy in targeted domains. Previously, during my internship at BIGO, I proactively built an AI chatbot to help the CEO’s office stay updated on the latest tech developments. Before transitioning into AI, I worked as a UX designer with NGOs and startups while completing my undergraduate degree in English Studies (First Class Honours). My transition to AI and NLP was driven by a passion for technology and a desire to combine my background in linguistics, human-centered thinking, and design.

I believe that fostering diversity in AI—especially greater inclusion of women and underrepresented groups—is key to building systems that truly reflect and serve the needs of all communities.
